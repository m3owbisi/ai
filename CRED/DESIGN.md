Equal AI — Influencer Brief
What is Equal AI
Equal AI is India's fastest growing AI call assistant. It's an Android app that picks up your unknown calls for you. The AI introduces itself as your assistant, talks to the caller, finds out who they are and what they want, and sends you a clean summary.
You don't pick up unknown calls anymore. The AI does.
Quick facts to mention:
40 lakh+ users already
Crores of calls handled
Built in India
Available on Android, iOS coming soon
Free to download
Important Features:
Assistant can speak to callers in up to 10 Indian languages - Hindi, Hinglish, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Tamil, Malayalam, Gujarati and more 
User can choose which promotional calls to mute and which ones to receive
Choose from different voice options
Get real-time conversation of the call and live nudges
Receive call summary and recording after the call
